2026-07-20: Methodology note — Why 10 runs per image.
  Decision: Running each image 10 times per noise type.
  Reason: Single runs are insufficient for statistical validity.
  CPU load, temperature, and BO optimization path vary between runs.
  10 runs enables calculation of mean, standard deviation,
  and 95% confidence intervals — minimum for publishable claims.
  Total experiments per noise type: 100 images x 10 runs = 1,000
  Total Phase 1 experiments: 7 noise types x 1,000 = 7,000
  This is consistent with standard ML research methodology.
